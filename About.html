    • About

    I am Percy Mthimkhulu,Johannesburg based 3D Artist and illustrator currently doing my Honours degree at the University of the Witwatersrand.

    Ever since I could hold a pencil,I have been creating works of art.From pencil sketches to paintings and various experimentations along the way.I have created original pieces by hand and through design software during my academic career.I am a critical thinker,a fast learner and I adapt well to a lot of different scenarios.

    During my time at Wits I was able to gain knowledge from various principles within my degree.Theoretical frameworks such as Digital Art Theory,Film Visual and Performance Art,Screen Studies & Media Studies, have allowed me to broaden my scope on how I can utilise my skills better in my field.I also learned Game design, 2D & 3D Animation, 3D modelling and Interactive media.

    Outside university I am a member of Pinkscale Creative Agency.A team of resolute, energetic, and innovative creatives with a strong passion for marketing solutions, events management, and visual content creation.

    Furthermore,I am able to use different kinds of software to create creative, innovative, and original work. I am trained with Adobe Premiere Pro,Illustrator, Photoshop and just began with After Effects, Krita,Blender and Unity .

    Ultimately, I am always up for a challenge and working with others to explore, create and immortalise.
